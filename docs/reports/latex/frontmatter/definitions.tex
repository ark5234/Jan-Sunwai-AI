Civic Complaint & A citizen-reported issue related to municipal infrastructure, utilities, sanitation, public safety, or governance services. \\
Complaint Lifecycle & Ordered state progression of a complaint such as Open, In Progress, Resolved, and Rejected. \\
Status History & Auditable log of state changes with timestamp, actor identity, and note. \\
Authority Routing & Mapping from classified category to responsible authority identifier and escalation parent. \\
Triage Queue & Review queue for low-confidence AI outputs requiring administrator decision. \\
Service Area & Worker-specific geospatial zone defined by latitude, longitude, and radius in kilometers. \\
Auto-Assignment & Rule-based process selecting an eligible worker based on department, availability, and workload. \\
Escalation Loop & Background process that pushes unresolved complaints to higher authorities when SLA conditions fail. \\
Rule Engine & Deterministic keyword and scoring logic used to improve category stability before optional LLM reasoning. \\
Hybrid Pipeline & Multi-stage architecture combining vision extraction, deterministic rules, and selective reasoning model invocation. \\
Draft Generation & AI-assisted creation of formal grievance text from image evidence and extracted metadata. \\
Graceful Degradation & Controlled failure behavior where the API returns meaningful retryable errors without service crash. \\
Magic Number Validation & File integrity check that verifies image binary headers against extension and MIME type. \\
Public Transparency Feed & Anonymized read-only endpoint exposing selected complaint records for public visibility. \\
Readiness Probe & Endpoint validating dependency availability, especially MongoDB and model runtime health. \\
Rate Limiting & Request throttling control to mitigate abuse and resource exhaustion. \\
Token Hashing & Storage of reset-token digest instead of raw token to reduce credential leakage risk. \\
Canonical Taxonomy & Standardized department labels used across classifier output, dashboards, and routing. \\
Operational Governance & Administrative controls for approvals, transfers, notes, audit review, and reporting. \\
