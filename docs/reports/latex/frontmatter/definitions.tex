\textbf{Term} & \textbf{Definition} \\ \hline
\endhead
Citizen & An external public user who interacts with the system to submit civic grievances and track their status. \\ \hline
Worker & A field operative or assigned staff member responsible for physically addressing and resolving a grievance. \\ \hline
Department Head & A managerial role responsible for overseeing grievances within a specific municipal department. \\ \hline
Admin & A high-level system user responsible for global platform governance, worker approvals, and triage operations. \\ \hline
Civic Grievance & A formal complaint submitted by a citizen regarding municipal infrastructure or service failures. \\ \hline
Triage Queue & A dedicated administrative queue for reviewing and manually routing complaints where the AI classification confidence is low. \\ \hline
Confidence Score & A numeric value (0 to 1) indicating the vision model's certainty regarding its predicted department classification. \\ \hline
Sanitization & The process of cleaning and filtering user input payloads to prevent security vulnerabilities. \\ \hline
Graceful Degradation & The system's ability to remain partially functional and maintain the core workflow even when the AI models are unavailable. \\ \hline
