# Official Department & Role Hierarchy

This document outlines the official, real-world department structure and the internal chain of command for the Jan-Sunwai AI project.

## 1. Health Department
*   Medical officer of health (MOH)
*   Chief medical officer (CMO)
*   Chief sanitary inspector (CSI)
*   Sanitary inspector (SI)
*   Assistant sanitary inspector (ASI)

## 2. Civil Department (I, II, and III)
*   Chief engineer
*   Superintendent engineer
*   Executive engineer
*   Assistant engineer
*   Junior engineer

## 3. Horticulture
*   Director
*   Dy. Director
*   Assistant Director
*   Section officer
*   Assistant section officer

## 4. Electric (I and II)
*   Chief engineer
*   Superintendent engineer
*   Executive engineer
*   Assistant engineer
*   Junior engineer

## 5. IT Department
*   Director
*   Joint Director
*   Nodal programmer
*   Programmer
*   Assistant programmer

## 6. Commercial
*   Director
*   Joint director
*   Executive engineer
*   Assistant engineer
*   Junior engineer
*   Meter erector/meter reader

## 7. Enforcement (North & South)
*   Director
*   Joint director
*   Section officer
*   All area inspector

## 8. VBD Department (Vector Borne Diseases)
*   Medical officer of health (MOH)
*   Chief medical officer (CMO)
*   Chief sanitary inspector (CSI)
*   Sanitary inspector (SI)
*   Surveillance Worker

## 9. EBR Departments (Engineering & Building)

*   Junior engineer

## 10. Fire Departments
